About Us

  • History

Falcon Venture Ltd is one of the leading trading houses in Bangladesh working in the field of container board paper, packaging materials and related products since 1996.

We have been representing some of the major paper mills from the USA, Australia, Taiwan, Malaysia, Qatar, Oman and India. As an indenting house Falcon has introduced many quality paper items to the packaging industries and contributed to the overall growth of the packaging sector in Bangladesh.

Falcon takes pride in establishing a reliable market for its suppliers. It has established a strong network of satisfied customers throughout Bangladesh. Falcon has earned the trust of both its suppliers and clients through its sincere approach and professional commitments.

Falcon has been working with a team of dedicated and trained marketing executives who understands the needs of our customers and deliver what they promise.

At Falcon we make sure that our customers get only the very best services. We always offer competitive price and deliver the goods to our customers according to our commitment.

Falcon Venture Ltd started its journey as an indenting house of industrial paper and packaging products in a rented office at old Dhaka in 1996. Throughout its long journey in last twenty years Falcon has expanded its wings and diversified its business operations. Falcon is now operating its business from its own corporate office at new DOHS, Mohakhali, Dhaka.

Besides trading international paper in Bangladesh market Falcon has established its associate companies like; Falcon Packaging Solution in 2017 which deals with packaging and printing machinery, Pelican Venture International in 2017 which deals with printing paper, and Pelican Paper in 2020 which deals with local paper trading.

Falcon aims to expand its business reach in the international market through establishing a trading house in the Emirates.

Falcon has been bridging the Bangladeshi carton box manufacturers with the leading and reputed paper mills around the world. Commitment, sincerity and honesty are the core business values of Falcon.

Our effort is not limited to sourcing quality paper only, we also promise to provide quality service at every stage of customer dealings. At Falcon we emphasize more on the relation with our Customers than our profit.

Falcon believes commitment is the key to success in business. We always try to be stick to our principle. Our customers can rely on our commitment.

At every stage of our customer service we make sure that we provide only our very best.
